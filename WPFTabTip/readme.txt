TabTipAutomation will open TabTip in Undocked state, and move your app to make focused element visible.


you can change InputScope of an element to change which keyboard will be displayed:

InputScope="Default"
InputScope="Url"
InputScope="EmailSmtpAddress"
InputScope="Number"
